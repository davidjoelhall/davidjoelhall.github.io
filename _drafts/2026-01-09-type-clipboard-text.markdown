You know those forms that will not accept you pasting in something? Yeah, those ones. They're problematic.

I finally took the 10 seconds and created a macro with [Keyboard Maestro](https://www.keyboardmaestro.com/main/) to type out the characters on the clipboard. 

I then went back to the form, hit the macro, and tada!
