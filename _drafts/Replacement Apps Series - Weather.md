### Weather

iOS 8's stock weather app is a nice 1-2 punch of where you are and the current weather. You can also see the next 6 hours as well as the forecast for the next week. This is pretty great, that is if you're looking ahead in time. What happens if the weather changes? Cue [Dark Sky]( https://appsto.re/us/nhD1E.i)... 

Dark Sky excels in one really awesome way for me, notifications. The app notifies me when rain is expected, and it's *very* accurate. The app also gives you the forecast, as the stock app does. It even gives you a radar overview of the world where you can see the weather anywhere you wish.
